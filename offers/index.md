# Offers

A second kind of civil-society knowledge: **technology offers** (discounts and donations available to nonprofits). Same OKF format as the [meal-site resources](../resources/index.md), different `type` — showing the format generalizes.

These are a **small, abstracted sample** drawn from TechSoup's Verbose Knowledge Base. The public-facing facts are here; the full expert guidance (implementation depth, security posture, sustainability analysis) is maintained in the private VKB.

The four below are **complementary** — distinct capabilities (open public data, productivity, social media, project management) that a tool like a Stack Builder would assemble into a stack. That contrasts with *alternatives/substitutes* (shown among the meal-site resources), where two entries share a capability and you need only one. See the [civic profile](../docs/civic-profile.md) for how both relationships are modeled.

Offers extend the civic profile with two offer-specific keys under `x-civic`: an `offer` block (the discount/donation) and `provenance.vendor_url`.

- [Data Commons](data-commons.md) — open public data
- [Google Workspace for Nonprofits](google-workspace-for-nonprofits.md) — productivity suite
- [Buffer for Nonprofits](buffer-for-nonprofits.md) — social-media management
- [Asana for Nonprofits](asana-for-nonprofits.md) — project management
