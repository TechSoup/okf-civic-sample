# Offers

Technology offers — discounts and donations available to nonprofits — in the same OKF format as the [meal-site resources](../resources/index.md), with a different `type`. These are an abstracted sample from TechSoup's Verbose Knowledge Base; the [civic profile](../docs/civic-profile.md) explains how offer relationships (`complements` vs. `alternative`) are modeled.

* [Data Commons](data-commons.md) — Google's open knowledge graph of public data — demographic, economic, health, and more — in one queryable, machine-readable format.
* [Google Workspace for Nonprofits](google-workspace-for-nonprofits.md) — Free nonprofit tier of Google's productivity suite — Gmail, Docs, Drive, Meet.
* [Buffer for Nonprofits](buffer-for-nonprofits.md) — 50% discount on Buffer's social-media scheduling, analytics, and engagement tools.
* [Asana for Nonprofits](asana-for-nonprofits.md) — 50% discount on Asana's project-management tiers for nonprofit operations teams.
